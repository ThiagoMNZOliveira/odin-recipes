Brigadeiro

Description

A chocolate dessert ball.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons of semi sweet chocolate powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 package of granulated chocolate

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, over low heat, put the butter and mix it until its liquid.
  2. Add the chocolate powder and the condensed milk, and mix until its homogenous.
  3. Heat at low heat until its starts to loosen from the bottom of the pan.
  4. Wait to cool down, and take it to the fridge for 2 hours.
  5. After the 2 hours, take a little bit of the mixture on a spoon and mold it into a little ball, then pass it on the granulated chocolate.
