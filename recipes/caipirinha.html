Caipirinha

Description

The most popular brazilian drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 dose of cachaça(or vodka if you don't have cachaça)
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Peel the lemon, cut it into four parts, remove the pits and the white fillet from the middle of the lemon.
  2. Squeeze directly into the glass (or cocktail shaker) with the help of a shaker spoon, along with the sugar.
  3. Add the ice and the cachaça
  4. Shake well and place in a glass decorated with a lemon wedge.
  5. You can serve the caipirinha strained or not.

    6. Drink wisely
