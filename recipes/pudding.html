Condensed Milk Pudding

Description

A simple pudding prepared with condensed milk.

Ingredients

    Pudding

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 150ml of milk
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2ml of vanilla essence
  • half vanilla bean

    • Syrup

  • 100grams of sugar
  • 50ml of water

Steps

    Syrup

  1. In a pan, place the water and sugar. Bring to a boil and mix until you get a clear syrup.

    2. Dough

  2. Place the condensed milk and the milk in a bowl and mix it manually.
  3. Pass the yolks through a sieve, adding to the mixture until it is homogeneous.
  4. At last, add the vanilla essence and the vanilla bean.
  5. Place the syrup in the bottom of a 13 cm pudding mold and then the dough.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil, bake at 180 degrees Celsius and bake in a water bath for approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool to put in the fridge. Unmold after 12 hours.
